# Privacy Policy for EXE Executor
### Written by DevNull-l1r on 6/24/2026

EXE Executor values your privacy.
Thankfully, this application runs entirely locally on your machine and operates with the following privacy principles:

* **Zero Data Collection:**
This application does not collect, log, or track any personal information, usage metrics, or diagnostic data.
* **Zero Network Traffic**
This application runs completely offline. It does not connect to external servers, and it never uploads or transmits data over the internet.
* **Local Operation:**
All configuartions, logs, and processes exist strictly within your local desktop session and are destroyed or saved locally based on your preference.
